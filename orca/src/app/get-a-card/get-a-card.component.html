Get a Card

Online

Order an unregistered adult card online. These are the only ORCA cards that may be ordered online. Other card types require proof of eligibility.

By Mail

Order an adult or senior card by mail. Print and complete the form. A proof of age for senior or youth orders will need to be included with the order.

In Person

Buy all card types at your local transit agency. Adult cards can be bought at participating retailers.

Reduced Fare Cards

Proof of eligibility

  • Youth (age 6 to 18) requires proof of age. May be ordered by mail. Card cost is $5.
  • Senior RRFP* (age 65 or older) requires proof of age. Card cost is $3. May be ordered by mail.
  • Disabled RRFP* requires proof of disability or a valid Medicare card. Card cost is $3. Issued in person as a photo is taken.
  • Low income requires proof of income.
    • In King County contact King County Metro.
    • In Kitsap County contact Kitsap Transit.

*A Regional Reduced Fare Permit card is required for senior or disabled passengers paying a reduced fare. Medicare cardholders are eligible for the permit. Contact your local transit agency.
